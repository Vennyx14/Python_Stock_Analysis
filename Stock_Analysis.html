5. Din gii & Kt lun

5.1. Xu hng chnh

  • Trong giai on 01/01/2023 14/06/2025, gi c phiu V (Visa) tng t khong 229$ (ngy u) ln 353$ (ngy cui), tng ng tng li sut khong 154 %.
  • ng SMA20 v EMA20 phn ln nm di gi ng, cho thy xu hng tng chung.
  • V d: khi gi ct ln SMA20 vo khong thng 11/2023, sau tip tc i ln xc nhn tn hiu tng.

5.2.Giai on bin ng cao

  • Thng 04/2025 c bin ng (volatility) ln nht, khong 3% ( lch chun ca daily return).
  • Nguyn nhn kh thi:
    • Bt ng tch cc v kt qu kinh doanh Q2, vi ch s ti chnh vt k vng.
    • Chng trnh mua li c phiu quy m, tc ng mnh ln cung cu trn th trng.
    • Source:
      • https://www.investors.com/research/investing-action-plan/stock-market-trade-war-tesla-google-boeing-earnings/
      • https://www.reuters.com/business/finance/visa-quarterly-profit-rises-strong-card-spending-volumes-2025-04-29/
  • Phn ln cc thng u c volatility n nh di 1% - 1.5%, th trng tng i n nh, t dao ng ln.

5.3. xut khuyn ngh u t c bn

  • Mua (Entry) khi:

    1. Gi ct ln EMA20 km volume tng t bin.
    2. RSI ang vng 4050 (khng qu mua, vn c d a tng).
    3. Gi chm vng h tr gn SMA50.

  • Bn (Exit) khi:

    1. RSI vt 70 v gi chng li hoc hnh thnh nn o chiu.
    2. Gi ct xung SMA20 cnh bo xu hng tng ngn hn suy yu.
    3. Volatility t bin cao , c th do tin bt ng.

  • Gi (Hold) khi:

    • Gi nm gia EMA20 v SMA50, RSI dao ng trong 4555, volatility thp th trng sideway.

Tm li, trong nm va qua, c phiu V cho tn hiu xu hng tng tng th, vi cc giai on bin ng nh im vo thng 04/2025. SMA/EMA gip xc nhn trend, RSI cho im overbought/oversold, v volatility cho bit ri ro dao ng. Kt hp cc ch s ny, nh u t c th mua khi gi ct ln EMA20 + RSI < 50, v bn khi RSI > 70 hoc gi xuyn SMA20.
